Kafala App… Your Simple Way to Do Good

Baraka is an innovative charity app that connects sponsors and orphans. It makes the process of donation, sponsorship, and even gifting easier all in a simple, transparent, and impactful digital way.

  • Transparent & Direct Sponsorship

    Track the details of your sponsorships, donations, and the updates of the orphan you support with full clarity.

  • Simple & Seamless Experience

    An easy-to-use interface that allows you to donate or send gifts in just seconds.

  • Meaningful Digital Gift in Your Loved One’s Name

    Turn your sponsorship into a valuable gift receive a personalized digital message and certificate instantly.

  • Smart Notifications & Instant Updates

    Get notified the moment your sponsorship is completed or when you receive a message from the orphan.
